Expert Determination Report: Mr. B. Mak, Assessed under the JAC Framework

An analysis of the family court hearing concerning the child Abel, assessing judicial conduct against the JAC (Judicial Appointments Commission) Framework.

Executive Summary

This report details a systematic failure of due process during a family court hearing. A point-by-point analysis of 22 distinct legal and procedural errors reveals that the presiding judge consistently failed to apply statutory law, ignored critical evidence, and misunderstood fundamental legal principles.

Conversely, the maternal uncle, Mr. Ben Mak, demonstrated a level of legal knowledge, procedural understanding, and commitment to child welfare that was consistently superior to that of the court.
